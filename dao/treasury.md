# Treasury

A portion of every borrower fee flows to the Monstro DAO treasury. The treasury is the financial reserve of the DAO, controlled by the same 5-of-7 multisig that owns the Based Loans contracts. It funds ongoing development, ecosystem support, and future governance decisions made by MONSTRO token holders.

***

## Where fees come from

Two separate fees apply to every Based Loans borrower.

**Protocol fee (2.49%):** Deducted from the USDC the borrower receives when the loan opens. This fee is collected by the FeeDistributor contract and distributed across three tiers. It is paid regardless of whether the borrower buys back.

**Term fee (2.5% / 5% / 7.5%):** Added to the borrower's buyback cost. This fee is paid only if the borrower buys back their collateral. It goes entirely to the lenders who funded the loan as their yield. It does not flow to the treasury.

See [Fees and terms](../how-it-works/fees-and-terms.md) for the full breakdown of both fees.

***

## How the 2.49% protocol fee is distributed

The FeeDistributor splits the protocol fee across three tiers on every loan.

| Tier | Share | Recipients |
|---|---|---|
| Protocol tier | 1.30% | DAO treasury (0.95%) and Monstro Labs (0.35%) |
| Burn tier | 0.70% | MONSTRO buyback and burns (0.35%) and mUSDC vault (0.35%) |
| Growth tier | 0.49% | Per-asset ecosystem partners and integrators |

**Protocol tier** funds the operational expenses of the DAO and Labs: development, audits, oracle infrastructure, and ongoing maintenance.

**Burn tier** is routed through the [AutoBurnSplitter](../technology/contract-addresses.md) contract. Half goes to buying and burning MONSTRO tokens on the open market, reducing supply permanently. The other half is injected into the mUSDC vault, increasing the vault's yield for all mUSDC holders. Both actions are permissionless and automatic.

**Growth tier** is allocated per asset at listing time. Projects and partners who help build lender coverage for their token receive a share of fees generated by loans against that collateral, for a fixed duration. This is how Based Loans incentivises ecosystem growth without central coordination.

{% hint style="info" %}
The split within the burn tier (MONSTRO vs mUSDC) is adjustable by the DAO council. The tier amounts themselves are set by the DAO owner multisig.
{% endhint %}

***

## Who controls the treasury

The Monstro DAO treasury is controlled by the DAO's 5-of-7 multisig. No single signer can move funds unilaterally. Any disbursement from the treasury requires a majority of signers to approve the transaction.

Treasury spending decisions are subject to Monstro DAO governance, meaning MONSTRO token holders have a say in how accumulated fees are used.

***

## What treasury funds are used for

Treasury funds support the long-term health of the Based Loans protocol and the broader Monstro ecosystem. General uses include:

* Funding ongoing protocol development and security audits
* Supporting ecosystem grants and integrations
* Covering operational costs for oracle infrastructure and other services
* Funding future governance initiatives approved by the DAO

Specific treasury actions are governed by Monstro DAO processes. For full details on treasury management and wallet addresses, see the [Monstro DAO documentation](https://docs.monstrodefi.com).

***

## Summary

* The 2.49% protocol fee is deducted from borrowers at loan open and split three ways.
* Protocol tier (1.30%): DAO treasury and Monstro Labs, for operations and development.
* Burn tier (0.70%): MONSTRO buybacks and burns, plus mUSDC vault injections, automated via AutoBurnSplitter.
* Growth tier (0.49%): per-asset allocations to ecosystem partners for a fixed duration.
* The treasury is controlled by the 5-of-7 DAO multisig. No unilateral withdrawals are possible.
* Full treasury details and wallet addresses are published at [docs.monstrodefi.com](https://docs.monstrodefi.com).
