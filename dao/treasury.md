# Treasury

A portion of every borrower fee flows to the Monstro DAO treasury. The treasury is the financial reserve of the DAO, controlled by the same 5-of-7 multisig that owns the Based Loans contracts. It funds ongoing development, ecosystem support, and future governance decisions made by MONSTRO token holders.

***

## Where fees come from

Every loan on Based Loans carries a fixed fee, paid upfront by the borrower at loan open. The fee is determined by the loan term chosen: 15, 30, or 45 days each carry a different rate. See [Fees and terms](../how-it-works/fees-and-terms.md) for the full fee table.

When a loan resolves, the fee is distributed: a portion goes to the lenders who funded the loan as their yield, and a portion flows to the Monstro DAO treasury.

## Who controls the treasury

The Monstro DAO treasury is controlled by the DAO's 5-of-7 multisig. No single signer can move funds unilaterally. Any disbursement from the treasury requires a majority of signers to approve the transaction.

Treasury spending decisions are subject to Monstro DAO governance, meaning MONSTRO token holders have a say in how accumulated fees are used.

***

## What treasury funds are used for

Treasury funds support the long-term health of the Based Loans protocol and the broader Monstro ecosystem. General uses include:

* Funding ongoing protocol development and security audits
* Supporting ecosystem grants and integrations
* Covering operational costs for oracle infrastructure and other services
* Funding future governance initiatives approved by the DAO

Specific treasury actions are governed by Monstro DAO processes. For full details on treasury management and wallet addresses, see the [Monstro DAO documentation](https://docs.monstrodefi.com).

## Summary

* A portion of every borrower fee flows to the Monstro DAO treasury.
* The treasury is controlled by the 5-of-7 DAO multisig. No unilateral withdrawals are possible.
* Treasury funds support development, audits, and ecosystem growth.
* Spending decisions are subject to Monstro DAO governance by MONSTRO token holders.
