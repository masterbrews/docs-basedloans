# Supported collateral

Based Loans launches with 25 accepted collateral tokens across two categories: major assets and partner/memecoin assets. Every token on the list uses the same 50% loan-to-value ratio, meaning you borrow USDC equal to half the current value of whatever you deposit. The protocol is designed to serve both conservative lenders who prefer established assets and lenders who are comfortable backing memecoins.

## How collateral works on Based Loans

#### ✔ 25 tokens at launch

Based Loans opens with 13 major assets and 12 partner and memecoin assets. This range reflects the protocol's core audience: people who hold tokens they cannot easily borrow against elsewhere, and who need USDC without selling.

#### ✔ Flat 50% LTV across all tokens

Every supported token uses the same loan-to-value ratio. Deposit $1,000 worth of any listed token and borrow $500 USDC. There is no tiered system, no negotiation, and no discount for majors. The 50% ratio creates the same buffer for lenders regardless of which token is posted as collateral.

#### ✔ Lenders choose which tokens they back

Just because a token is listed does not mean every lender accepts it. Each lender configures which tokens they are willing to back and sets a USDC cap per token. If you hold a less common token, your loan will be matched to lenders who have opted into that token specifically.

#### ✔ New tokens are added over time

The initial list of 25 is a starting point. Tokens that meet the listing criteria, including verified contracts, sufficient on-chain trading activity, and oracle availability, can be added through the asset management process. See the get your token listed page for the full criteria.

***

Based Loans is built for the full spectrum of the Base ecosystem. Whether you hold a major token or a memecoin, if it is on the list, you can borrow against it without selling it.
