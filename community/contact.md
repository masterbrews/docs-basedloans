# Contact

The Based Loans team is reachable through X and Discord. Different channels work better for different types of questions.

***

## General support

If you have a question about a loan, a deposit, or how something works in the protocol, the fastest path is the [Discord](https://discord.gg/monstrodefi) community. Post in the relevant channel and someone from the team or community will respond.

For quick public questions, you can also reach out on X at [@based\_loans](https://x.com/based_loans).

***

## Token listing inquiries

If you represent a project and want to get a token listed on Based Loans as collateral, start by reading the [Get your token listed](../supported-collateral/get-your-token-listed.md) page.

To start a conversation, DM [@based\_loans](https://x.com/based_loans) on X, or reach out through Discord. Include the token name, contract address, and a brief description of the project.

***

## Partnerships and integrations

For partnership discussions, integrations, or anything that does not fit the categories above, DM [@based\_loans](https://x.com/based_loans) on X. For more involved conversations, Discord works as a starting point for getting connected with the right person on the team.

***

## Security disclosures

If you have found a potential vulnerability in the protocol, do not post it publicly. Contact the team through a private DM on X at [@based\_loans](https://x.com/based_loans) with a description of the issue. The team will respond promptly and work with you on responsible disclosure.
