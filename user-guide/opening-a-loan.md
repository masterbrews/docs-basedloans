# Opening a loan

You need: a supported collateral token in your wallet and ETH for gas.

***

## Step 1: Open the Borrow page

Click **Borrow** in the top navigation. If you have not opened a loan before, you will see the new borrower screen. Click **Select collateral** to begin.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-1.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 2: Choose your collateral

Select the token you want to borrow against. Only supported tokens appear here.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-2.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 3: Set your loan terms

Enter how much collateral you want to deposit and choose your loan term: 15, 30, or 45 days. The interface shows the USDC you will receive and your buyback cost. You cannot change these after the loan opens.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-3.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 4: Review and confirm

Check all the details: collateral amount, USDC you receive, protocol fee, buyback cost, and expiry date. Click **Confirm** when ready.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-4.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 5: Approve token access

The protocol needs permission to transfer your collateral. Sign the approval transaction in your wallet.

This only happens once per token.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-5.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 6: Open the loan

Sign the loan transaction in your wallet. Your collateral is transferred and USDC is sent to your wallet.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-6.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 7: Loan opened

You will see a success screen confirming your loan is live.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-7.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 8: Your borrow dashboard

Your active loan appears on the borrow dashboard with your buyback cost, collateral amount, and expiry date. The buyback cost is fixed and will not change.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/ug-borrow-8.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

{% hint style="warning" %}
Set a reminder before your loan expires. Once the term ends, buyback is no longer possible and your collateral transfers to the lender.
{% endhint %}
